BGMI 4.0 Update Highlights

BGMI ka 4.0 update, jise **Spooky Soiree** naam diya gaya hai, Halloween aur festive season ko dhyan mein rakhte hue naye aur interesting features le kar aaya hai. Is update se game ka experience aur bhi rochak ho gaya hai.

Major Features:

  • **Stepwell**: Erangel map mein ek naya, India-inspired battleground joda gaya hai jo vertical combat aur sniping ke liye perfect hai.
  • **Ghost Gameplay**: Eliminated teammates ab "bhoot" ban kar wapas aa sakte hain aur apni team ko heal ya scan abilities de sakte hain.
  • **Spooky Soiree Themed Mode**: Is naye mode mein players ko haunted mansion explore karne ka mauka milega.
  • **Naya Weapon**: Is update mein ek naya explosive weapon **Mortar** bhi shamil hai.
  • **Unfail 4v1 Mode**: Yeh ek naya survival mode hai jisme ek hunter ko chaar survivors ko shikaar karna hota hai.
  • **Diwali Events**: Players festive events aur rewards ka bhi anand le sakte hain.

Ye sabhi naye features BGMI ko ek naya aur mazedaar experience de rahe hain. Is update ke features ko aur detail mein dekhne ke liye, yahan click karein.
